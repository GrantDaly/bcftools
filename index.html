Methods

  • Samtools Calling ("bcftools call -c")
  • Multiallelic calling ("bcftools call -m")
  • BCFtools/trio-dnm calling ("bcftools +trio-dnm2")
  • BAQ calculation
  • Samtools math notes
  • SegBias calculation
